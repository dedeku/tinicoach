## 16. MVP PRIORITÁS

- ✅ Auth (register, login, password reset)
- ✅ Journal (CRUD, list, stats)
- ✅ Habits (CRUD, 1 habit for free)

- ✅ PWA setup (manifest, service worker)
- ✅ Dashboard (home screen)
- ✅ Feature gates (Free vs Premium)

- ✅ Strength Quiz
- ✅ Community Voting
- ✅ Video library
- ✅ Profile & Settings

### Phase 3 (Nice to Have - Week 4):

- ✅ Push notifications
- ✅ Onboarding flow
- ✅ Data export (GDPR)
- ✅ Email notifications
- ✅ Install prompts

# Product Mission

## Pitch

tinicoach is a solution-focused teen life coaching Progressive Web App that helps Hungarian teenagers (13-18 years old) develop emotional resilience, build positive habits, and discover their strengths by providing guided journaling, habit tracking, and personalized coaching content in their native language.

## Users

### Primary Customers

- **Parents of Teenagers (13-18)**: Parents seeking positive mental health support tools for their teens, particularly those concerned about stress management, self-esteem, and personal development during adolescent years.

- **Direct Users (Teenagers)**: Hungarian-speaking teens aged 13-18 who are looking for accessible, judgment-free tools to manage stress, build confidence, and develop positive daily habits.

## The Problem

### Mental Health Support Gap for Hungarian Teens

Hungarian teenagers face increasing levels of stress, anxiety, and low self-esteem during critical developmental years. Traditional mental health services are often expensive, have long wait times, and carry social stigma. Meanwhile, most available digital wellness tools are in English, creating a language barrier that reduces effectiveness and engagement.

Current alternatives include:
- **Traditional therapy**: Expensive (15,000-25,000 HUF/session), limited availability, 2-3 month waiting lists
- **Generic wellness apps**: English-only, not culturally adapted, lack solution-focused approach
- **School counseling**: Limited hours, often reactive rather than proactive

This results in teens lacking accessible, affordable, and culturally relevant tools for daily emotional wellness and personal development.

**Our Solution:** tinicoach provides affordable, always-accessible, solution-focused coaching tools in Hungarian that teens can use daily to build resilience, track progress, and develop positive habits - without the barriers of cost, wait times, or language.

## Differentiators

### Solution-Focused Approach

Unlike traditional therapy apps that focus on problems and diagnosis, tinicoach uses solution-focused brief therapy principles that emphasize strengths, goals, and future possibilities. This approach is more engaging for teens and produces faster results.

### Hungarian Language & Cultural Relevance

Unlike international wellness apps (Headspace, Calm, Fabulous), tinicoach is built specifically for Hungarian teens with culturally relevant content, local idioms, and understanding of the Hungarian educational system's pressures.

### Privacy-First with GDPR Compliance

Built with European data protection standards, including special protections for users under 16 (parental consent), ensuring parents can trust the platform with their teens' sensitive data.

### Freemium Model with Immediate Value

Free tier provides genuine value (3 journal entries/week, 1 habit tracker) allowing teens to build the habit before asking for premium upgrade, unlike competitors who paywall basic features.

### Progressive Web App Flexibility

No app store required - instant access via web browser with offline capabilities. Users can later download from app stores when Capacitor version launches. This removes friction from initial adoption.

### Coach-Managed Content

Unlike static apps, tinicoach allows the coach to regularly update daily messages, community questions, and video content through a CMS, keeping the experience fresh and responsive to user needs.

## Key Features

### Core Features

- **Gratitude Journal:** Write and reflect on three things you're grateful for daily, track mood patterns, and build emotional awareness through guided prompts
- **Habit Tracker:** Set up to 10 daily or weekday habits, track completion with visual streaks, and build consistency with motivational reminders
- **Strengths Finder Quiz:** Discover your top 5 character strengths through a research-based assessment and learn how to leverage them in daily life
- **Dashboard:** View your weekly progress at a glance, see habit completion rates, mood trends, and quick access to daily actions

### Engagement Features

- **Community Voting:** Participate in weekly community questions, see how other teens are thinking, and feel connected without social comparison pressure
- **Video Library:** Access solution-focused coaching videos on topics like stress management, confidence building, and healthy relationships
- **Daily Motivational Messages:** Receive inspiring push notifications tailored to teens' daily challenges and growth opportunities

### Premium Features

- **Unlimited Journaling:** Write journal entries as often as needed without weekly limits
- **Multiple Habit Tracking:** Track up to 10 habits simultaneously vs 1 on free tier
- **Premium Video Content:** Access to full video library with deeper coaching on specific challenges
- **Repeat Strengths Assessment:** Retake the strengths quiz quarterly to track personal growth
- **Priority Support:** Direct access to the coach for questions and guidance

### User Management Features

- **Age-Appropriate Auth:** Email/password registration with automatic age detection and parental consent workflow for users under 16
- **Flexible Subscription:** Monthly or annual premium plans via Stripe with transparent pricing (2,990 HUF/month or 29,990 HUF/year)
- **Data Export:** GDPR-compliant data export feature allowing users to download all their journal entries and personal data
- **Onboarding Flow:** Welcoming multi-screen introduction that explains features and encourages PWA installation for best experience
